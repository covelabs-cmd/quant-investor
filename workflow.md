This document provides a simple example of how a research project can be organized.

## Step 1: Define the research question

Write a clear and testable research question.

## Step 2: Collect data

Document the data source, collection date, and any preprocessing assumptions.

## Step 3: Run experiments

Keep scripts, notebooks, and outputs organized.

## Step 4: Review results

Summarize findings, limitations, and next steps.

## Step 5: Reproduce

Make sure another user can understand the workflow from the documentation.
